What is Starcraft?

Starcraft is a series of games in the RTS (Real Time Strategy) genre

The most important game in the series though is the first expansion for Starcraft 1,
Starcraft: Broodwar

Starcraft is why E-sports exists today. The games popularity in South Korea caused it to explode into mainstream media in the country. Matches would be shown live on TV channels and Starcraft pro's were respected athletes with massive followings. It is a phenomena that is only now, nearly 20 years later, starting to surface in the western regions of the world.
