So what is DOTA 2?

DOTA 2 or "Defence of the Ancients 2" is also a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena)

It's a step up in complexity compared to League though...

The original Defence of the Ancients was the game that League of Legends was inspired by and it is these two franchiese which domiante the MOBA genre in gaming. The gameplay is functionally the same but the philosophies of the two games are wildly different. DOTA 2 is an infinitely complex game whereas while it isnt 'easy' by any stretch, League of Legends is definitely known for attracting a slightly more casual player base.
