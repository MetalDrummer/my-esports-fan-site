So what is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena)

It's a team effort

League of Legends is a game where teamwork is absolutely vital and while it is possible or one player to shine and really carry their team, it is nearly always the more cohesive team that is victorious. Players play as a single "champion" in a game and the elite players often become known for playing specific chmapions.
