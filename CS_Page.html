So what is Counter Strike?

Counter Strike is a series of games in the FPS (First Person Shooter) genre

The most important game in the series though is the latest interation,
Counter Strike: Global Offensive

While Counter Strike may not have been the first "First Person Shooter" it is certainly the one that has endured the longest and the one which has left the biggest impact on e-sports. It is a game so simple a 10 year old can understand its mechanics yet fiendishly difficult to master. If League of Legends, DOTA 2 and Starcraft Broodwar players are the 'nerds' of e-sports then Counter Strike pro's are almost certainly the "jocks".
